Scrambled Eggs

This recipe will teach you how to make scrambled eggs. To begin, you will need:

  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Salt and Pepper

Use a pan and follow these steps to make scrambled eggs.

  1. Melt butter in pan
  2. Crack eggs into pan of melted butter
  3. Break the yolk and constantly stir and scramble eggs as they cook
  4. Add salt and pepper while eggs are cooking

Enjoy your scrambled eggs
